Lasagna

This lasagna is very yummy. Wow it looks so great :) Who doesn't love a classic lasagna? Modern Honey got the recipe you.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small Onion
  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 3/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder (or 5 garlic cloves, finely minced)
  • 3/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Pepper
  • 32-40 oz jar of Marianara Sauce (Depends on how "saucy" you want it)
  • Paramesan Ricotta Filling
    • 2 cups of Whole Milk Ricotta Cheese
    • 1 cup of grated Parmesan Cheese
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon of salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
    • 12 lasagna noodles
    • 4 cups of mozzarella cheese (may use more if you want it extra cheesy

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and cook for about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add ground beef and increase heat to medium-high.
  5. Cook until the meat begins to brown, about 5 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  7. Paramesan Ricotta Filling
    1. In a small bowl, stir together ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, egg, basil, salt, and pepper.
    2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
    3. Spread the bottom of a 9 x 13 baking pan with Â¼ cup of the meat sauce.
    4. Place 3 noodles in a single layers on top of the sauce.
    5. Spread each noodle with parmesan ricotta cheese filling and sprinkle with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and meat sauce.
    6. Repeat. Finish with remaining mozzarella
    7. Spray a large piece of foil with non-stick cooking spray and cover the lasagna.
    8. Bake for 15 minutes and then remove the foil.
    9. Continue to bake until the cheese if bubbling, about 25 minutes longer.
    10. Cool the lasagna for 10 minutes before cutting into pieces.
